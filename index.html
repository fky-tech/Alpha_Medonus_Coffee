Our Heritage

Ethiopian Coffee Legacy

In Ethiopia's fertile highlands, where coffee was born, we cultivate exceptional beans with generations of expertise. As a family-owned business, we honor traditions while innovating to meet global standards.

Our collective of farmers—family and neighbors alike—tend each plant with meticulous care. The volcanic soil, ideal altitude, and perfect climate combine to create coffee with unparalleled complexity and character.

We partner with discerning buyers worldwide who value traceability, quality, and the story behind each bean. Our coffee carries the soul of our community—a testament to generations of dedication to this craft.

Our Coffee Products

Quality

Coffee quality is the result of a careful, intentional process that spans across multiple stages, each one influencing the final cup.

We give great attention from the very beginning, attention to detail is crucial, ensuring that the plant is nurtured with care and that it produces cherries of the highest potential.

Consistent

At the heart of great coffee is consistency—ensuring that each step is executed with care, expertise, and attention to detail.

So we can prepare the same level of quality of coffee every year. This ensures traceability and quality every single crop we produce. We engage in long-term supplying of our coffee with our clients.
